Hey, I'm Tanat
Game Developer

I'm passionate about making games engaging and impactful

I'm a student at Rochester Institute of Technology studying game design and development. I have worked primarily with C#, Unity, and MonoGame (the spiritual successor to XNA). I like exploring different gameplay algorithms to create an experience that is both fluid and interactive. I also like to experiment with different design concepts in atempt to evoke a wide range of emotions.

Outside of programming, I enjoy learning more about the human complex, either through the rationality defined in game theory or the social interactions examined in social psychology. I also work as a graphic designer to help further understand the emotions that are perceived from different styles and designs.
